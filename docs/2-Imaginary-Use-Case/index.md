# The Story of a Community Network

Imagine this: you arrive at a small community center in a rural area. There's a building with a few classrooms, an office, and a courtyard. Across the street, there's a library. A few hundred meters away, a second building hosts workshops and a small clinic.

**There is no internet.**

The community asks you: *"Can you help us get connected?"*

This chapter tells the story of what happens next. It follows a fictional — but entirely realistic — journey of building a community network from scratch. Each section represents a **challenge** you'll face along the way, and how you might solve it.

!!! tip "How to use this chapter"
    Read it like a story. Each section builds on the previous one. By the end, you'll have a mental model of what a complete community network looks like.

    When you're ready to actually **build**, head to [Chapter 3 — The Guide](../3-Guide/index.md) for step-by-step technical instructions.
