# *"Something broke and I'm 500 km away"*

You built the network during a two-week visit. Now you're back home, 500 km away, and Zabbix just sent you an alert: a router needs reconfiguring.

How do you reach it? You can't SSH into a device behind a NAT from the outside — not without a **VPN** (Virtual Private Network).

A VPN creates a secure tunnel between you and the community network, no matter where you are. It's like being physically there, except you're on your couch.
