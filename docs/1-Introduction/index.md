# Introduction

Welcome to the **Community Network Handbook** — a practical guide to building and running community networks from scratch, even with limited resources.

This chapter explains **why** this handbook exists and **who** it's written for. If you're eager to get your hands dirty, feel free to skip ahead to [Chapter 2](../2-Imaginary-Use-Case/index.md) — but we recommend spending five minutes here first.

!!! tip "How to read this book"
    The handbook is divided into four parts:

    1. **Introduction** — You are here. Motivation and context.
    2. **Imaginary Use Case** — A fictional story that walks you through building a community network step by step, from the first router to a full deployment.
    3. **Guide** — Detailed, step-by-step technical instructions for every technology mentioned in the story.
    4. **Real Use Cases** — Field deployments where these ideas have been applied in the real world.

    We recommend reading Chapter 2 (the story) first, then jumping into Chapter 3 (the guide) for the specific technologies you need.
